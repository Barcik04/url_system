# Settings and Profile

## How does a signed-in user open Settings?
1. Click the three-line menu icon in the top-right corner of the navigation bar.
2. Select **Settings** from the menu.

## How does a user change their avatar?
1. Open **Settings**.
2. In the middle of the screen, find the large gray circle avatar area.
3. Hover over the avatar area.
4. Click **Edit Avatar**.
5. Choose an image from the computer.
6. The avatar will be updated.
7. The user can change the avatar again later.

## How does a user delete their account?
1. Open **Settings**.
2. Find the **Delete account** section.
3. Click the red trash can button next to the delete option.
4. Enter the current password to confirm deletion.
5. After confirmation, the account is permanently deleted.

## What happens after account deletion?
Account deletion is irreversible. The deleted account cannot be recovered.
