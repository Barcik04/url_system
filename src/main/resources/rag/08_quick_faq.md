# Quick FAQ

## How do I register?
Click **Sign up**, enter your email and password, then verify the account through the email message.

## How do I sign in?
Click **Sign in**, enter your account details, and submit the form.

## How do I create a short URL?
Click the application logo, fill in the URL form, optionally set an expiry date, and click **Create URL**.

## Can I create a short URL without signing in?
Yes, but you must copy and save it immediately because it will not appear later in a personal dashboard.

## Where can I see my URLs?
Signed-in users can see their URLs in the **Dashboard**.

## How do I edit or delete my URL?
Open the **Dashboard**, hover over the chosen URL, then use the **pencil** icon to edit or the **trash can** icon to delete.

## How do I change my avatar?
Open **Settings**, hover over the avatar circle, and click **Edit Avatar**.

## How do I buy Premium?
Open **Settings**, then **Subscriptions**, and click **Buy** on the Premium plan.

## How do I cancel Premium?
Open **Settings**, then **Subscriptions**, and click the red **Cancel subscription** button.

## How do I delete my account?
Open **Settings**, go to **Delete account**, click the red trash can button, and confirm with your current password.
