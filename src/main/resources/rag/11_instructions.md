# For not signed in users

## How to register
-- On the navbar at the top of the screen click "signup"  
-- Insert your email and create a strong password  
-- Account verification email will be sent to you, so to create an account you need to verify it by clicking a button in sent email  
-- After verifying you are ready to sign in

## How to sign in
-- On the navbar at the top of the screen click "sign in"  
-- Insert you account details (the same during registration)  
-- YOu are ready to use features explicit to signed in users!

## How to create Url
-- Click our application logo in the top-left corner on the screen  
-- Youll see a template with a field to insert your email that you want to convert  
-- you can also set expiry date for this URL (optional)  
-- When everything is ready then click "Create URL"  
-- On the bottom, new short url will be shown, please copy it and store because if youre not a signed in user, you wont be able to find this URL ever again

# For signedin users

## Dashboard

### How to access dashboard
-- On the navbar at the top of the screen click three stripes in the top-right corner, the menu will appear  
-- Select Dashboard from the menu  
-- You are met with all your URLs created by you

### How to edit/delete URLs
-- You are able to delete or edit your URLs  
-- To do that you need to focus your mouse on the particular URL you want to operate on  
-- Then on the right side, next to chosen URL delete button (trash can) and edit button (pencil) will appear  
-- If you click delete youll have to confirm deletion  
-- If you click edit, youll be met with edit URL form with fields like URL (insert new URL to substitute for the old one) and expiry date (you can update expiry date of this URL too)  
-- If you dont want to update any of the fields then leave it empty

## Admin panel

### How to access and operate in Admin panel
-- On the navbar at the top of the screen click three stripes in the top-right corner, the menu will appear  
-- Select "Admin" from the menu  
-- You are met with a panel of all urls existing in the system.  
-- You can edit,delete, create and filter them the same as in the normal user dashboard

## Settings

### How to change your avatar
-- On the navbar at the top of the screen click three stripes in the top-right corner, the menu will appear  
-- Select "Settings" from the menu  
-- In the middle of the screen youll see a big gray circle  
-- When you hover on it youll see a button "Edit Avatar"  
-- By clicking it can choose any picture from your computer and the avatar will be set (you can change it later)

### How to buy a PREMIUM subscription
-- On the navbar at the top of the screen click three stripes in the top-right corner, the menu will appear  
-- Select "Settings" from the menu  
-- In the middle youll see a big "Settings" text  
-- below that there are different settings and one of them is "Subscriptions"  
-- Click "open" next to "Subscriptions"  
-- Youll see two subscription plans: Regular and Premium  
-- To buy premium you need to click "Buy" button of a template of a subscription you want to buy  
-- If youre unhappy with PREMIUM plan you can click red "cancel subscription" button and your subscription will be canceled and will not be billed again unless you decide to resume it.

### How to delete an account
-- On the navbar at the top of the screen click three stripes in the top-right corner, the menu will appear  
-- Select "Settings" from the menu  
-- In the middle youll see a big "Settings" text  
-- below that there are different settings and one of them is "Delete account"  
-- Click a red trash can next to "delete button"  
-- Youll be asked to insert your current password to confirm deletion.  
-- Your account is irrecoverably deleted
