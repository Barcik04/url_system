# Subscriptions

## How does a user open the subscriptions section?
1. Open **Settings**.
2. Find the **Subscriptions** section below the main Settings heading.
3. Click **Open** next to **Subscriptions**.

## What subscription plans are available?
The application has two subscription plans:
- Regular
- Premium

## What is the link limit for the Regular plan?
The Regular plan allows up to 10 links.

## What is the link limit for the Premium plan?
The Premium plan allows unlimited links.

## How does a user buy the Premium plan?
1. Open **Settings**.
2. Open the **Subscriptions** section.
3. Review the available subscription plans.
4. Click **Buy** on the Premium plan card.

## How does a user cancel the Premium subscription?
1. Open **Settings**.
2. Open the **Subscriptions** section.
3. Click the red **Cancel subscription** button.

## What happens after canceling the Premium subscription?
After canceling the Premium subscription, it will not be billed again unless the user decides to resume it later.
